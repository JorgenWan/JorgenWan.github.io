Problems 

  • Cop Robber Game. In 2019, Cox and Sanaei made two conjectures: (1) For any given r∈(0,1), there is a series of graphs such that the ratio of the damage number and the capture time approaches r. (2) For Paley graph P_n , the damage number equals (n-1)/2 when n≥13. I proved Conjecture 1 and I also proved Conjecture 2 in the case that n=13. Here is my Mathematics undergraduate thesis for these problems.
